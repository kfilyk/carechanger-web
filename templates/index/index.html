A practical care solution for medical personnel.

The CareChanger platform is a wheelchair embedded sensor for detecting void events among incontinent wheelchair users, coupled with a communication and application service for care providers.

Real-time data collection for optimal patient care.

Temperature and humidity sensors in the device collect data in real-time. The device notifies caretakers of an event as it occurs, allowing for an immediate response.

Easy Wi-Fi connectivity.

Our devices conform to 802.11b/g/n Wi-Fi standard, and can be utilized up to 400 meters away. Multiple devices can be quickly calibrated to operate on your local care network.

Live, automatically updated trend graphs.

With real-time data collection, a graph of all past patient void events are immediately generated on the web app, allowing caretakers to track patient trends and activities.

Scalability perfected.

With the ability to manage several patients and devices within discrete caregroups, CareChanger is both flexible and sensitivite to the needs of caretakers and the privacy of clients.
